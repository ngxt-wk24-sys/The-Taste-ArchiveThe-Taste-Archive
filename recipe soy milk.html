Soy Milk

Ingredients

  • Soybeans 30 g
  • Water 500 ml

Step 1

Rinse the soybeans thoroughly and soak them in water for 6–8 hours until softened.

Step 2

Drain the soybeans, then blend them with fresh water until smooth.

Step 3

Strain the mixture if a smoother texture is preferred.

Step 4

Pour the soy milk into a pot and heat over low heat while stirring, simmer for 3–5 minutes once it boils, then remove from heat and add sugar if desired, or enjoy plain for the natural soy flavor.
