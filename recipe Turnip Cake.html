Turnip Cake

Ingredients

  • White radish / daikon 500 g (peeled and grated)
  • Rice flour / glutinous rice flour 100 g
  • Water 200–250 ml
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms 3–4 (soaked and chopped)
  • Chinese sausage / dried shrimp to taste (chopped, optional)
  • Spring onion / scallion 2 stalks (chopped)
  • Salt & white pepper to taste
  • Oil a little

Step 1

Grate the white radish or cut into coarse strips (coarse strips enhance the radish flavor), add salt, and let sit for 10 minutes to release water.

Step 2

Squeeze out excess water from the radish, stir-fry in a little oil until softened, then add mushrooms, sausage, or dried shrimp and sauté until fragrant.

Step 3

Pour the mixture into a greased steaming tray and steam over medium heat for 40–50 minutes until set.

Step 4

Remove from steamer, let cool, cut into pieces, and serve directly or pan-fry until golden on both sides.
