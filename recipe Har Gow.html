Har Gow

Ingredients

  • Fresh shrimp 150 g (chopped or minced)
  • Lard / vegetable oil 1 tsp
  • Spring onion / scallion 1 stalk, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • White pepper a little
  • Sugar ¼ tsp (optional)
  • Cornstarch / potato starch 1–2 tsp
  • Dumpling wrappers / Har Gow wrappers enough

Step 1

Prepare the filling: Mix shrimp, scallions, salt, white pepper, sugar, cornstarch, and oil until combined.

Step 2

Take a wrapper, place a spoonful of filling in the center, pleat the edges by hand, leaving the top slightly open.

Step 3

Line the steamer with cloth or lightly oil it, place the dumplings inside.

Step 4

Steam for 6–8 minutes until shrimp is cooked and the wrapper is slightly translucent.Remove from steamer and serve hot with soy sauce or chili sauce.
