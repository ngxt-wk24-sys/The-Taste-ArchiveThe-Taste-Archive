Braised Egg

Ingredients

  • Eggs 6–8
  • Water enough to cover the eggs
  • Black tea leaves 2–3 tsp
  • Star anise 2–3
  • Bay leaves 2–3 (optional)
  • Soy sauce 3–4 tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Sugar 1–2 tsp (optional)
  • Optional spices: cinnamon, Sichuan peppercorns, etc.

Step 1

Place eggs in a pot, add water to cover, and boil on high heat for 8–10 minutes until fully cooked.

Step 2

In the same pot, add black tea leaves, star anise, bay leaves, soy sauce, salt, and sugar, then pour enough water to make a tea-spice broth.

Step 3

Remove the eggs and gently crack the shells with a spoon without peeling them.Place the cracked eggs into the tea-spice broth, simmer on low heat for 1–2 hours so the flavor penetrates the eggs.

Step 4

After cooking, let the eggs soak in the broth for several hours or overnight for stronger flavor.
