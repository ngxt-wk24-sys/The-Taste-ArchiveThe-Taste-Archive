Hong Kong Milk Tea

Ingredients

  • Hong Kong–style black tea leaves 8–10 g
  • Water 500 ml
  • Evaporated milk 120–150 ml
  • Sugar to taste (optional)

Step 1

Prepare boiling water and keep the induction cooker on to maintain heat.Place 50 g of black tea powder into a tea bag, put it in a cup or pot.Pour boiling water into the tea bag, letting the water hit the tea powder to release flavor, while the induction cooker keeps heating the tea at the bottom.

Step 2

Continue pouring and brewing at the same time; the tea powder gradually releases its flavor and the tea becomes strong.

Step 3

Remove the tea bag, add evaporated milk and sugar if desired, and stir well before serving.
