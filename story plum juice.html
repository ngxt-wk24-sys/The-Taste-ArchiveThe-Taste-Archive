Food Story

Plum Juice

Originated in Southern
China
especially Guangdong and Fujian.

Plum juice is a traditional drink rooted in everyday life and practical wisdom. Made by slowly simmering smoked plums with herbs and sugar, it offers a refreshing balance of sour and sweet that helps cool the body and cut through rich foods.

Over time, it became a familiar presence in kopitiams and street stalls across Southeast Asia. For many, plum juice is more than a beverage—it is a taste of childhood, summer afternoons, and comforting memories shared across generations.
