Mint Tea

Ingredients

  • Tea bag (black or green tea) 1 bag
  • Fresh mint leaves 5–8 g
  • Water 300–400 ml
  • Honey or rock sugar to taste (optional)

Step 1

Rinse fresh mint with stems and leaves, and place in a cup.Add a green tea bag.

Step 2

Pour about 200 ml of boiling water over the mint and tea bag, and steep for 3–5 minutes

Step 3

Remove the tea bag and add honey to taste.Stir gently and serve; can be enjoyed hot or chilled.
