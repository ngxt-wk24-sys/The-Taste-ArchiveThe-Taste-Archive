Classic Morning

Youtiao with Century Egg and Pork Congee is crispy, savory, and a classic traditional breakfast.

Savory
Crispy

This combination is a timeless masterpiece of comfort food, celebrated as the "Golden Duo" of Chinese breakfast. The soul of Century Egg and Pork Congee lies in the slow-cooking process, where rice grains simmer for hours until they break down into a velvety, silk-like texture. The savory essence of tender pork seeps into every spoonful of the congee, while the bits of century egg melt slightly under the heat, releasing a rich, distinctively earthy aroma. The crowning touch is the freshly fried Youchar Kway (oil cruller), which brings an airy crispness that elevates the entire experience. The ultimate magic happens in the three seconds after the cruller is dipped into the bowl: the outer layer soaks up the savory, creamy congee and becomes succulent, while the core retains its stubborn crunch and fragrant oiliness. This contrast of "soaked yet crispy" and "silky yet firm," catalyzed by a dash of spicy white pepper, transforms humble grains into a multi-layered symphony of flavors, providing a profound sense of warmth and satisfaction that soothes the soul.

A bowl of silky warmth, a bite of golden crunch. Find comfort for the soul and peace in the city.
