Siu Mai

Ingredients

  • Ground pork 150 g
  • Cooked rice 50 g
  • Fresh shrimp 80–100 g, chopped
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms 2–3, soaked and chopped
  • Spring onion / scallion 1 stalk, chopped
  • Light soy sauce 1–2 tbsp
  • Cooking wine / rice wine 1 tsp
  • Salt & white pepper to taste
  • Sesame oil a little
  • Siu Mai wrappers / wonton wrappers – enough

Step 1

Prepare the filling: Heat a little oil in a pan, stir-fry mushrooms and scallions until fragrant, then add pork and shrimp and stir-fry 1–2 minutes.

Step 2

Add soy sauce, cooking wine, salt, white pepper, and sesame oil, and mix quickly.

Step 3

Add cooked rice and mix well so that the rice is evenly distributed in the filling.

Step 4

Take a wrapper, place a spoonful of filling in the center, gather the edges around the filling leaving the top open, and gently press the bottom.

Step 5

Place siu mai in a steamer and steam 8–10 minutes until fully cooked.Remove from steamer and serve hot with sweet or chili sauce.
