Sweet & Savory Duo

Siu Mai meets Red Bean Soup, a fresh take on traditional dim sum.

Savory
Crispy

In the midst of rising steam, the pairing of Siu Mai and Red Bean Soup creates an enchanting melody of savory and sweet on the streets of KL. The Siu Mai offers a grounded satisfaction; its paper-thin skin wraps around succulent meat and bouncy shrimp, delivering a direct punch of savory richness and firm texture. Beside it, the Red Bean Soup provides a gentle contrast. Simmered until velvety and smooth, the subtle fragrance of dried tangerine peel cuts through the sandy sweetness of the beans, washing away any lingering oiliness. This interplay of "savory bite and sweet delight" allows the palate to dance between the firm and the fluid, the bold and the elegant. It is more than just a meal; it is a philosophy of life—that within the heavy rhythm of the day, there is always room for a soft, sweet finish.

Savory bites, sweet delights. A simple blend of life's true warmth.
