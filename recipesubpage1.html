Braised Pork Rice

Ingredients

  • 500g pork belly
  • 1 onion
  • 8 shiitake mushrooms
  • 20g rock sugar
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 star anise
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 slices ginger
  • Eggs (as needed)
  • Five-spice powder (as needed)
  • White pepper powder (as needed)
  • Cooking wine (as needed)

Step 1

Wash and dice the pork belly and shiitake mushrooms (üí°The skin of the pork belly must be kept‚ÄºÔ∏è to bring out the full collagen protein flavorüòà).

Step 2

Slice the white onion into thin strips. Heat oil in a pan, add the onion strips, and pour in enough oil to cover the onions. Fry over low heat for 15-20 minutes, or until the onions turn golden brown. Remove from the pan.

Step 3

(üí°The heat must not be too high‚ÄºÔ∏èIf it burns, it will taste bitter and ruin the whole pot of braised pork~ Fried onions are the essence of braised pork rice~)

Step 4

No need to wash the pan. Leave a small amount of oil in the pan and stir-fry the diced pork belly over medium heat until the fat renders out and the surface of the pork turns golden brown.

Step 5

Add diced shiitake mushrooms, minced garlic, and ginger slices, and stir-fry until the mushrooms release their juices. Add cooking wine, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, rock sugar, star anise, fried shallots, and plenty of water (üí° at least 1 cm above the meat‚ÄºÔ∏è the sauce for braised pork rice shouldn't be reduced too much, so add plenty of water here). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 1-1.5 hours.

Step 6

We can boil some eggs, peel them, and then add them to the braised meat to stew together.

Step 7

Stir evenly so that every grain of rice is coated with the broth, then enjoy!
