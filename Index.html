Discover the taste of the past

Welcome to Old-School Chinese Flavors — a place that helps you rediscover classic Chinese foods.Here, we collect many familiar tastes such as pineapple buns, fried dough with soy milk, rice dumplings, aiyu jelly, grass cakes, and more. Our goal is to help young people and the public learn about the stories and culture behind these foods. Whether you are trying them for the first time or looking to relive childhood memories, you will find your own “old-school flavor” here.
