Teh C

Ingredients

  • oose black tea leaves 2–2.5 g
  • Hot water 200 ml
  • Evaporated milk 30–40 m
  • Sugar 10–15 g

Step 1

Add loose black tea leaves, sugar, and hot water into a shaker. Close the lid and shake for about 10–15 seconds to extract the tea quickly.

Step 2

Add evaporated milk, then shake lightly or stir until well combined.

Step 3

Pour and serve (add ice if desired).
