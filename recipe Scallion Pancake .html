Scallion Pancake

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour 200 g
  • Hot water 100–120 ml (~50–60℃)
  • Salt ½ tsp
  • Scallions / green onions 2–3 stalks, chopped
  • Oil for kneading and frying

Step 1

Mix flour and salt, gradually add hot water, stir with chopsticks to form crumbs, then knead into a smooth dough.

Step 2

Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 20–30 minutes.

Step 3

Divide the rested dough into small portions, roll out into thin sheets, brush lightly with oil, and sprinkle chopped scallions.

Step 4

Roll the sheet into a log, coil into a spiral, then gently flatten into a round pancake.

Step 5

Heat a little oil in a pan over medium heat and fry the pancake until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
