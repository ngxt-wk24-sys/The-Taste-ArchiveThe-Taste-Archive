Oyster Omelette

Ingredients

  • Fresh oysters 100 g
  • Eggs 2
  • Sweet potato starch / tapioca starch 1 tbsp
  • Water 2 tbsp
  • Green onions 1, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • White pepper a pinch
  • Cooking oil as needed
  • Sauce (optional: chili sauce or sweet soy sauce) a little

Step 1

Clean the oysters and drain. Mix sweet potato starch with 2 tbsp water to make a smooth slurry.

Step 2

Heat some oil in a pan over medium heat. Add green onions and stir-fry briefly.

Step 3

Pour the egg-starch mixture into the pan. Place oysters on top. Cook 2-3 mins until the bottom sets and lightly golden. Flip carefully and cook another 1-2 mins.

Step 4

Transfer to a plate. Serve with chili sauce or sweet soy sauce if desired.
